Beyond Status Quo Blog

The Public Speaking Sphere—whether that’s in debate or MUNs, has largely marginalized many groups of people, from men dominating the arena leaving women involvement to be a lot less comparatively to people part of the LGBTQ+ community, or those that are differently-abled (speech-wise, cognitively etc). In hopes of sharing stories of people of what it means to have these experiences and any other they would like to share, we started our blog to empower others facing the same challenges but afraid to raise their voices.
