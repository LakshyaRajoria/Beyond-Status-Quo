    Welcome to Beyond Status Quo

    Working together to promote discourse on pertinent topics, reaching those of less privilege,
    and learning more about the power of each of our voices.

    Beyond Status Quo

    We realized that there isn’t any platform in India right now that would allow one to see all the opportunities to showcase
    their talents of argumentation or even to experience and learn from events that are happening around them. Thus, in the hopes of bringing to others a platform for debating and creating connections throughout our school, city, state and one day throughout the country we created Beyond Status Quo.

    Beyond Status Quo

    Through our platform users can create, administrate,
    and participate in tournaments across India.
